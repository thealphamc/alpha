Minecraft:
Prison Escape

It took 3 months to write, design, publish, and market the book.

An Unofficial Minecraft Comic Book.

From AlphaMC, I bring to you "Minecraft: Prison Escape," a comic book inspired by the Minecraft prison that propelled Alpha's channel to fame. With stunning Minecraft scenes, puzzles, and easter eggs, this short, action-packed comic brings an immersive experience for all gamers to enjoy.

In late 2021, Alpha's first prison video went viral, gaining millions of views, skyrocketing his channel from 300 to 10K subscribers in under a week! By consistently improving videos, he soon reached 100K subscribers and released a Minecraft comic as a tribute.
