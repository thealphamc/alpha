FAQ

Questions I get asked a lot, whether it be Minecraft-related or just about myself.

How old are you?

I'm currently 15. Just counting up the days until until I'm 18... Or should I?

What Minecraft shaders do you use in your videos?

I mainly use Sildur's Enhanced Default, which you can download here.

What's your gaming setup?

All my equipment can be found on the home page of my website.

How do I search up your YouTube channel?

Search "AlphaMC" or type in my handle, @AlphaMC. Since my channel name, "Alpha," is pretty vague, most search results typically revolve around Gen Alpha and Alpha males.

How much ad revenue do you earn?

Usually around $100-$1K monthly, as I upload only a few videos per month.

Why don't you post very often?

Over the past few years, YouTube has been primarily a hobby as I'm still in high school, but I do have plans to create more content in the future.

Do you have a life?

Probably not. But ironically, I don't watch television or play any video games.

Do you have a Discord server?

Yes, I am active on my server and you can ask me questions there too. My Discord username is alphamc.

Do you have a Minecraft server?

Yes, I do host Minecraft events on my Discord server. For an SMP server, however, you can join Diamond222.minehut.gg and type /join Diamond222 in the chat.

When did you first start playing Minecraft?

Back when I was about 7 years old, I explored the demo world on the Xbox 360. Seeing how cool the blocks were, I begged my parents to buy the full version. Eventually they did, but they took away my Xbox priveleges if I played for too long.
